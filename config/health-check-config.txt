Health Check Protocol: HTTP
Health Check Path: /health
Health Check Port: Traffic port (80)

Interval: 30 seconds
Timeout: 5 seconds
Healthy Threshold: 5 consecutive successes
Unhealthy Threshold: 2 consecutive failures
Success Codes: 200

Rationale:
The /health endpoint allows the load balancer to automatically detect
unhealthy instances and remove them from traffic rotation without manual intervention.
